Japanese money is used in Japan.

The yen is the official currency of Japan.
It is the third-most traded currency in the foreign exchange market,
after the United States dollar and the euro.
It is also widely used as a third reserve currency after the US dollar and the euro.

Series E banknotes were introduced in 2004 in ¥1000, ¥5000, and ¥10,000 denominations.
